Officials say participation in Sunday's regional elections exceeded the previous cycle by a wide margin, with turnout figures still being finalized in several districts.

Election officials attributed the surge in participation to expanded early-voting access and a series of high-profile local ballot measures that drew significant public attention in the weeks leading up to polling day.

Full results are expected within the coming days as mail-in ballots are counted. Several races remain too close to call, and at least two districts have indicated a recount may be requested once final tallies are confirmed.

Observers noted that youth turnout in particular appeared to rise compared with prior cycles, based on preliminary exit polling conducted at a sample of polling stations.
